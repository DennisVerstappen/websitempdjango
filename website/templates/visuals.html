Visuals

My music was mostly inspired by movies, art or some kind of imagery. To convey the feeling to the listener of the music, I made images that could serve as backgrounds for my YouTube uploads. After a while I thought still images were a bit boring and I wanted to create some animations to accompany the music and enhance the listening experience. This resulted in several attempts to create a audio reactor, a video that reacts to music. After learning more about creating these videos, the next logical step was animating whole sequences. This progress is captured in the Revelations EP.

Armed with the knowledge I gained about animations and videos I started doing these live at parties. At these events I trigger visuals on the fly as a reaction to the music the DJ plays. Also, I provide parties with custom visuals which can play on loop so the background on the stage or some place else in the venue is filled up.
